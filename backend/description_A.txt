This database supports the SenseSaaS Retail Analytics platform. It organizes information around Customers, Products, Orders, and Reviews, enabling comprehensive analysis of sales performance and customer behavior.

GLOBAL SCHEMA NOTES:
- **Multi-tenancy**: All tables include a `CompanyId` column. Data MUST be filtered by `CompanyId` for the current tenant.
- **Soft Deletes**: All tables include an `IsDeleted` column. Records with `IsDeleted = 1` should be excluded from active queries.

Key entities include:
- **Customers**: Individuals who purchase products.
- **Products**: Items available for sale, categorized by type.
- **Orders**: Transactions made by customers, including status and totals.
- **OrderItems**: Detailed line items for each order.
- **Reviews**: Customer feedback and ratings for products.
- **Inventory**: Stock levels for products across different locations.

IMPORTANT BUSINESS LOGIC:
- **Revenue**: Calculated as the sum of `TotalAmount` from the `Orders` table where `Status` is 'Completed'.
- **Customer LTV**: Sum of `TotalAmount` for all completed orders for a specific customer.
- **Top Selling Products**: Determined by summing `Quantity` in `OrderItems` joined with `Orders` (completed).
- **Date Handling**: All dates are stored in UTC.

# Customers
Description: Stores customer profiles including contact info and joining date.
PK: CustomerId
FKs: CompanyId

# Products
Description: Catalog of products with pricing and category information.
PK: ProductId
FKs: CompanyId

# Orders
Description: Transactional records of customer purchases.
PK: OrderId
FKs: CustomerId -> Customers(CustomerId), CompanyId

# OrderItems
Description: Individual items within an order.
PK: OrderItemId
FKs: OrderId -> Orders(OrderId), ProductId -> Products(ProductId), CompanyId

# Reviews
Description: Product reviews and ratings submitted by customers.
PK: ReviewId
FKs: ProductId -> Products(ProductId), CustomerId -> Customers(CustomerId), CompanyId

# Inventory
Description: Current stock levels for products.
PK: InventoryId
FKs: ProductId -> Products(ProductId), CompanyId
